Chapter 2 Rate of Change & Tangent Line

2.1 Solved Problems

At the beginning of Calculus 1, you probably will work on Rate of Change and Tangent Line of a Curve. The following are problems you might encounter.

Given the following function, find the average rate of change over the given intervals.

\(f(x)= 10x^3 +10 \qquad a)[5,7] \quad b)[-3,3]\)

For solving this problem, I am going to use the slope formula, which can take many form. \[ m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}\\ m = \frac{f(x_2) - f(x_1))}{x_2 - x_1}\]

Time to do the Math

\[m = \frac{f(x_2) - f(x_1))}{x_2 - x_1}\\ m = \frac{f(5) - f(7)}{5 - 7}\\ m = \frac{10x^3 + 10 - 10x^3 + 10}{5 - 7}\\ m = \frac{10(5)^3 + 10 - 10(7)^3 + 10}{5 - 7}\\ m = \frac{1260 - 3440}{5 - 7}\\ m = 1090 \]

With that said, the slope is 1090 on the interval [5,7]
